As explained in “best_hospitals.txt”, hospital score percentage ranking is used to make hospital scores of different scale easier to compare. The correlation coefficient between average score percentage ranking and average HCAHPS score (Base score + Consistency Point score) is -0.30867050920041444, which points to a weak negative linear relationship.The correlation coefficient between variation of score percentage ranking and average HCAHPS score (Base score + Consistency Point score) is -0.10357199640349998, which indicated there is not a linear relation between these two variables.
